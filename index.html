Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about influencer marketing in India

What is influencer marketing and how does it work?

Influencer marketing is a form of social media marketing that involves partnerships between brands and content creators to promote products or services to their engaged audiences. It works by leveraging the trust and authenticity that influencers have built with their followers, making it more effective than traditional advertising.

How much does influencer marketing cost in India?

Influencer marketing costs in India vary based on follower count, engagement rates, and content type. Nano-influencers (1K-10K) typically charge ₹500-₹2,000 per post, micro-influencers (10K-100K) charge ₹1,000-₹10,000 per post, while macro-influencers charge ₹25,000-₹1 lakh+ per post. Location also matters - Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore influencers may charge premium rates.

How do you choose the right influencers for campaigns?

We use data-driven analysis to match influencers based on audience demographics, engagement rates, content quality, brand alignment, and authentic engagement patterns. Our AI-powered tools analyze factors like audience location (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.), age groups, interests, and engagement quality to ensure perfect brand-creator fit.

What's the average ROI of influencer marketing campaigns?

Our campaigns typically generate 4:1 to 8:1 ROI, with an average of ₹6 return for every ₹1 spent on influencer marketing. ROI varies by industry, campaign goals, and execution quality. Fashion and beauty brands often see higher ROI (6-10x), while B2B campaigns typically see 3-5x returns.

Do you work with micro-influencers and nano-influencers?

Yes, we work with creators across all tiers - from nano-influencers (1K-10K followers) to mega-influencers (1M+ followers). Micro and nano-influencers often provide the highest engagement rates and most authentic connections with their audiences, especially in specific Indian cities and niches.

What platforms do you cover for influencer marketing?

We specialize primarily in Instagram marketing, which is the most popular platform for influencer marketing in India. We also work with YouTube creators and can coordinate campaigns across multiple social media platforms based on your target audience and campaign objectives.
